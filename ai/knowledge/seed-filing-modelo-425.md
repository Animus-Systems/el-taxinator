# Modelo 425 — Filing procedure

_Last verified: 2026-01-01 (seed)_
_Agency portals change — verify steps against the official help before filing._

## Overview

- Annual recap of IGIC — summarises the four quarterly Modelo 420 filings into a single informative declaration.
- Frequency: annual. Statutory deadline: **1 to 30 January** of the year following the exercise (e.g. Modelo 425 for 2026 is filed 1–30 Jan 2027).
- Non-payment: the 425 is purely informativa — any amounts owed were already paid via the quarterly 420s.

## Portal

- Agency: **Agencia Tributaria Canaria (ATC)**.
- Sede electrónica: <https://sede.gobiernodecanarias.org/tributos/jsf/publico/index.jsf>
- Filing service: "Presentación de autoliquidaciones → Modelo 425 — Declaración-resumen anual".

## Login options

- **Certificado digital** (FNMT, SL, persona física).
- **Cl@ve permanente / Cl@ve PIN** for autónomos.
- Autofirma required on macOS/Windows for certificate signing.

## Step-by-step

1. Log in at the ATC sede, navigate to **Modelo 425 — Resumen anual**.
2. Select the **ejercicio**.
3. The form prefills cumulative totals from your four submitted Modelo 420 filings — verify each trimestre row.
4. Fill **Volumen total de operaciones** (annual taxable sales + exempt/non-subject if applicable).
5. Distribute sales by IGIC rate: 0% / 3% / 7% / 9.5% / 15% / 20% / 35%.
6. Fill IGIC deducible total and any compensación pendiente at year-end.
7. If there's a **devolución** pending (accumulated compensación with no future activity), request it on the dedicated screen.
8. Validate and **Firmar y enviar**.

## Payment / result

- No payment on 425 — it's strictly informative.
- Download the **justificante** and keep with the annual cierre (SL) or libros del IGIC (autónomo).
- If a devolución is claimed, ATC processes it in 30–90 days (may be longer around campaign peaks).

## Common issues

- Mismatch between sum of four 420s and the 425 totals: the form flags it; usually caused by a retroactive complementaria on one of the quarters — re-import and reconcile.
- "Operaciones con partes vinculadas": disclose if intercompany sales above threshold exist.
- Exempt operations (exportaciones fuera de la UE, operaciones intracomunitarias) have dedicated casillas — verify your entity's exports are there.
