# Modelo 100 — Filing procedure

_Last verified: 2026-01-01 (seed)_
_Agency portals change — verify steps against the official help before filing._

## Overview

- Annual personal income tax declaration (IRPF / "Renta"). Every individual subject to Spanish IRPF with income above the filing threshold must file.
- Frequency: annual. Statutory campaign window: roughly **early April to 30 June** (exact dates published each year by AEAT). Direct debit (domiciliación) typically closes 5–7 days earlier (~25 June).
- Consolidates: employment income, autónomo activity, rental income, crypto disposals (base del ahorro), dividends/interest, and deductions.

## Portal

- Agency: **AEAT**.
- Filing tool: **Renta Web** (browser-based) — no desktop software needed.
- Sede path: <https://sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es/> → "Renta <YYYY> — Campaña" → "Servicio tramitación borrador / declaración (Renta Web)".

## Login options

- **Cl@ve PIN** — simplest, works for most residents.
- **Cl@ve permanente** — set up ahead of the campaign.
- **Certificado electrónico / DNIe** — allows filing for a spouse jointly.
- **Número de referencia** — generated on the spot from your prior year's casilla 505 and current DNI/NIE support — AEAT-only lightweight option.

## Step-by-step

1. Open Renta Web from the Sede, authenticate.
2. AEAT pre-populates a **borrador** with known data (payroll, dividends, retenciones, pension contributions, etc.). Review every block.
3. **Rendimientos del trabajo**: cross-check payslips' gross + IRPF retained; apply the €2,000 flat deductible gastos.
4. **Actividades económicas** (autónomos): enter rendimiento neto from the libro de ingresos/gastos; consistent with quarterly Modelo 130 cumulative ingresos/gastos.
5. **Rendimientos del capital inmobiliario** (rentals): enter rent, apply 60% reduction on long-term residential, list deductible expenses.
6. **Rendimientos del capital mobiliario**: dividends, interest, staking rewards, P2P lending.
7. **Ganancias y pérdidas patrimoniales**: crypto FIFO gains, stock sales, fund sales, real estate. Import via Modelo 721 cross-reference where applicable.
8. **Reducciones y deducciones**: pension plan contributions (pensions capped at €1,500 own + employer match), donations, autonómicas (Canarias-specific deductions apply here).
9. Save → Validate → preview **resultado**.
10. Choose "Presentar declaración".

## Payment / result

- A pagar (positive): three options:
  - **Ingreso directo** via NRC.
  - **Domiciliación bancaria** (cut-off typically 25 June — five days before the general deadline).
  - **Fraccionamiento** in two payments: 60% now, 40% by 5 November (no extra interest if via domiciliación).
- A devolver (negative): provide IBAN; AEAT typically refunds within 30–90 days, sometimes up to 6 months.
- Download the **justificante** — save it, AEAT may audit up to 4 years later.

## Common issues

- Crypto disposals missing from borrador: AEAT doesn't auto-populate foreign exchange data → you must add them manually. Use our Modelo 721 report as the source.
- Joint vs. individual filing: run the simulator in Renta Web; usually individual is cheaper for two earners above ~€20k each.
- Canarias deductions (familia numerosa, vivienda habitual, donaciones, etc.) are on a separate screen — easy to miss.
