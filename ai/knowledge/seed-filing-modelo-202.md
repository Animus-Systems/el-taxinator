# Modelo 202 — Filing procedure

_Last verified: 2026-01-01 (seed)_
_Agency portals change — verify steps against the official help before filing._

## Overview

- Quarterly "pago fraccionado" of Impuesto sobre Sociedades (IS) for entities that trigger the obligation (turnover > certain threshold, or those who opted in to the "modalidad de base imponible").
- Frequency: quarterly. Statutory deadlines: **1 to 20 April**, **1 to 20 October**, **1 to 20 December** (only three — no January/August filing).
- Two calculation options: (a) % on prior year's cuota integra, or (b) % on current year base imponible ("modalidad opcional") — the second must be elected via Modelo 036 in February.

## Portal

- Agency: **AEAT**.
- Sede electrónica: <https://sede.agenciatributaria.gob.es/>
- Direct path: "Impuestos y tasas → Impuesto sobre Sociedades → Modelo 202 — Pago fraccionado".

## Login options

- **Certificado electrónico de persona jurídica** — strongly recommended; Cl@ve works for personal representative logins but not for SL as the legal entity directly.
- **DNIe of the legal representative** also valid.
- Certificate must be pre-installed in the browser trust store or via Autofirma.

## Step-by-step

1. Open the AEAT sede and search "Modelo 202".
2. Click "Presentación ejercicio <YYYY>" and authenticate with the SL's certificate.
3. Confirm **NIF** and **razón social**.
4. Select **periodo**: **1P** (April), **2P** (October), **3P** (December).
5. Select the calculation modality:
   - **Modalidad 1** (Art. 40.2 LIS): 18% of the prior year's cuota integra adjusted for deductions.
   - **Modalidad 2** (Art. 40.3 LIS): current-year base imponible × rate (typically 17%, minus retenciones/pagos a cuenta prior).
6. Fill casilla 01 (base imponible or importe de la cuota), 02 (tipo de gravamen), 03 (cuota íntegra).
7. Casilla 04 — pagos a cuenta de periodos anteriores en el mismo ejercicio.
8. Casilla 05 — a ingresar.
9. Click **Validar**, then **Firmar y enviar**.

## Payment / result

- Positive (almost always): **NRC from the SL's business bank account** or **domiciliación** (cutoff 15th).
- Zero: file as zero — non-filing is a sanction even when nothing is due, if the SL has the obligation.
- Download the **justificante** after submission; store in the company's accounting archive.

## Common issues

- Wrong modality: changing between 1 and 2 mid-year requires prior Modelo 036 update; otherwise the AEAT rejects with a tipo/base mismatch warning.
- Missing the April 1P period because the SL wasn't aware it had the obligation: expect a sanction + recargo; file retroactively via complementaria.
- For new SLs in first fiscal year: no modalidad 1 possible (no prior cuota); use modalidad 2 only.
